IDLI

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 tablespoon fenugreek seeds
  • 5 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 1/2 cup urad dal
  • salt as required
  • water as required

Recipe

  1. Add urad dal & methi seeds to a bowl. Add rice or idli rava to another bowl.
  2. Wash them very well separately until water runs clear. Soak them separately in lot of water for at least 6 hrs.
  3. Rinse and soak poha with ¼ cup water for about 30 mins before blending.

Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cups long grain rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • 3 quarts water, Salt
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 – 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 sprig curry leaves

Recipe

  1. Prepare Dosa Batter
  2. Process rice and urad dal mixture in commercial
    grinder or food processor.
  3. Prepare Masala Dosa
